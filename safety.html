Safety Net

Real-Time Alerts for Safer Roads – AI-powered accident prevention using V2V
A project by Sara Saju

Safety Net is an in-built vehicle safety feature that uses AI, deep learning, and Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication to predict and prevent accidents in real time — especially on high-risk roads like Bengaluru’s KIA Airport corridor.

The Problem

  • India accounts for 9.5% of the world’s 1.2 million annual road deaths
  • Bengaluru 2023: 899 fatalities → one death every 4 days
  • KIA road remains one of India’s deadliest stretches
  • Overspeeding causes 35% of fatal accidents
  • Rain + waterlogging dramatically increases crash risk

Solution & Key Features

  • Real-time analysis of weather, historical accidents, flight schedules & traffic density
  • Continuous driver behaviour monitoring (speed, braking, steering, G-forces)
  • Deep learning predicts accident-prone zones within 50 m
  • V2V broadcasts overspeeding or sudden braking to nearby vehicles
  • Clear voice + HUD alerts: “Caution: Accident-Prone Area Ahead” | “Alert: Nearby vehicle overspeeding”

Risk Identification & Driver Alerts

Impact

A proactive safety net that turns every equipped car into part of a shared early-warning system — with the potential to save hundreds of lives per year on just one dangerous road.
